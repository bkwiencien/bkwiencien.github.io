A musing on waiting times in coin flipping

Flip a fair coin until the sequence (hh) occurs. Flip a fair coin until the sequence (ht) occurs. At first blush one would think the expected waiting times for these events are the same. After all, the probability of the sequence (hh) = the probability of the sequence (ht) = .25, but the expected waiting time is 6 and 4 respectively. See: penny-anty

In order to demonstrate this, we will flip the metaphorical coin until the required sequence occurs ( either hh or ht). We will perform that experiment a configurable number of times and then look at what that stats tell us
