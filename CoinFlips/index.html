A musing on waiting times in coin flipping

Flip a fair coin until the sequence (hh) occurs. Flip a fair coin until the sequence (ht) occurs. At first blush one would think the expected waiting times for the event are the same. After all, the probability of the sequence (hh) = the probability of the sequence (ht) = .25, but the expected waiting time is 6 and 4 respectively. See: penny-anty
